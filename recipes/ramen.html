Hakata Tonkotsu Ramen

Description

An easy to follow, restaurant style Tonkotsu Japanese Ramen. Listed below are the ingredients and steps you need to make your own delicious bowl for an enjoyable evening.

Courtesy of RAMEN KAONASHI

Ingredients

    Noodles
  • 400g Flour
  • 116ml Water
  • 5g Kansui
  • 5g Salt

    • Ramen Sauce タレ
  • 30g Dried shrimp
  • 2pc Rishiri kombu
  • 3pc Dired Shiitake Mushrooms
  • 300ml Water
  • 80ml Light soy sauce
  • 20ml Thai fish sauce
  • 8ml HON MIRIN
  • 40ml Cooking Sake
  • 10ml Rice vinegar
  • 35g Sea salt
  • 30g Sliced garlic
  • 30g Sesame seeds
  • 20g Oyster sauce

    • Ramen soup Broth
  • 1pc Pre-boiled pork head bone
  • 1pc Pre-boiled pork bone
  • 3L Water
  • 500g Pork shoulder loin

    • Chashu チャーシュー
  • 50ml Koikuchi soy sauce
  • 50ml Cooking Sake
  • 1tbsp Sugar

    • Topping
  • 50ml Hon mirin
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 1.5 tbsp Japanese chilli powder
  • 1pc Spring onions
  • 1pc Chashu
  • 1pc Sliced black fungus
  • 1pc Ajitama

    • Hakata Tonkotsu Ramen
  • 30ml Ramen sauce
  • 300ml Tonkotsu soup
  • 110g Noodles
  • 1 set All toppings

Steps

    Ramen Noodles
  1. Add flour 400g to mixing bowl. Mix with water, kansui and salt. Stir the kansui mixture into the flour. Place into resealable bag and flatten. Set aside for 40mins. Cut into thin noodles.
    2. Ramen Sauce タレ
  2. Add dried shrimp, cut kombu, dried shiitake and water to a container. Cover the dashi and leave in fridge overnight. Add dashi to the pot and boil. Once boiling, simmer for 5mins. Strain dashi. Add dashi, usukuchi shoyu, thai fishi sauce, hon mirin, cooking sake, rice vinegar, sea salt, sliced garlic and sesame seeds to cooking pot. Bring to boil and stop after the sauce boils. Add oyster sauce. Add the sauce to a blender after cooling it. Blend until smooth.
    3. Ramen Soup Broth
  3. Add pre-boiled pork head bone, pre-boiled pork bone and water to large pot. Boil it at medium heat. Remove scum. Change to low heat and add pork shoulder loin, simmer for 3 hours. Remove pork shoulder loin to use for chashu. Change to high heat to boil for 1 hour. Stop fire. Strain to separate 600ml of soup and leave aside. Refill water to the large pot so the the water level reaches the same as the start. Boil again at high heat. Continue boiling for 2-3 hrs then stop. Strain soup to separate 600ml of soup to same pot earlier. Leave it aside to cool. Refill the large pot again with water. Start to boil at high heat for 2-3 hrs. Strain the remaining 700ml of soup.
    4. Chashu
  4. Add koikuchi shoyu, cooking sake and sugar into a sauce pan. Boil sauce until cooking sake fully evaporates and set aside to cool. Marinate pork shoulder loin with chashu sauce for 1 hr.
    5. Toppings
  5. Add Hon mirin, cooking sake and sugar to sauce pan. Boil until the sugar cameralises. Added Japanese chilli powder and stir to mix well. Chilli paste is done. Slice srping onions for garnish. Slice chashu to add for ramen. Add sliced black fungus, chilli paste, menma, ajitama and the rest of toppings.
    6. Finale
  6. Add ramen sauce and tonkotsu soup into a bowl. Boil noodles for 1min
